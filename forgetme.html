Forget-Me-Not

The Summary

A multimedia design that is targeted to keep students from procrastinating, while giving them an incentive to do their work. There is an app that is linked to the watch that connects to the internet every six hours and downloads basic assignment data from connected school websites such as BBLearn. It also stores data offline for easy access anywhere. The app sends out notifcations to the watch "x" amount of times before the assignment is due. Finally, the app tracks when assignments are turned in, and what grades are returned, giving rewards accordingly.

Testing

The group did multiple testings and research on people using a google survey, which helped give us information on procrastination.

We came up with multiple ideations to help brainstorm a final design for our product.

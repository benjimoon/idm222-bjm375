Doma Website Redesign

The Home Page

This is a prototype redesign for Doma Restaurant's website that I made from scratch using Figma. Before the redesign, the website was really lacking in aesthetic, culture, and overall imagery which made the website look less professional. I took it upon myself to redesign the home page, the menu page, and the about us page.

The About Us Page

This is the about us page that I created because customers are bound to be curious about the history and culture of Doma. This page will create a better connection between Doma and the community of Philadelphia.

The Menu Page

This is the menu page that I recreated for Doma because their old menu page used a third party source for their menu. Having a custom menu page on their website, will create less confusion for users.
